About Me

My name is Sofija Kitanovikj, a driven and ambitious 20-year-old student with a passion for knowledge and personal growth. Born with a creative eye and a thirst for learning, I have always been dedicated to expanding my horizons and exploring various fields of study such as photography, graphic design and computer science.

Currently, I am pursuing an undergraduate degree at the University of Ss. Cyril and Methodius, at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering in Skopje. The rapid and immense advancement of technology and computer science has sparked a deep fascination within me, leading me to pursue a career exactly in this field, while also being able to use my creative side and creative thinking.

By nature I am a valuable and dedicated person, filled with a zest for life, bringing a touch of joy and enthusiasm to everything I do.

Photography

My photography journey started back in elementary school and ever since it has been my gateway to preserving and cherishing the different life moments, by freezing time in a single frame.

Beyond the techincal aspects, photography for me is an extension of my very being, an intimate glimpse into the corridors of my soul and the prism through I perceive the world.
Through my photographs, I reveal a piece of myself, inviting viewers to step into my vision and connect with my emotions.
